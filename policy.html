Security Policy

1. The contribution and dissemination of prohibited information and negative materials that are against the policies of the State are prohibited.
2. Disseminating political, inflammatory or racist information or anything that affects the reputation of the National Library of Malaysia and the Public Service is prohibited.
3. All information to be uploaded to the National Library of Malaysia website must be approved by the Head of Department/Division.
4. Any acts of trespassing and attempts to hack the National Library of Malaysia website are prohibited.

Privacy Policy

Information Collected

No personal information is collected while visitors browse this website except for information submitted by you via e-mail. This information may be used by other public departments/agencies to assist in the provision of more effective services. For example, such as in resolving inquiries or complaints that require feedback from other agencies.

Log File

In order to improve the level of security and efficiency of the National Library of Malaysia's website, general visitor information such as browser type, domain name, country name and other information will be used in the collection and analysis of demographic information and the pages frequently visited by customers. However, it does not involve individual privacy.

Link

This website contains links to other websites. This privacy policy applies only to this website. It should be noted that linked websites may have different privacy policies and visitors are advised to review and understand the privacy policies of each website visited.

Inquiries or Complaints

Any questions or complaints about this privacy policy can contact us via webmaster@pnm.my
